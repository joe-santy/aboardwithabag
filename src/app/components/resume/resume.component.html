About me

I hold a bachelor's degree in International Business and a master's degree in Education. I love to travel and have lived in seven different countries over the past dozen years. Having worked as a teacher and as a consultant, I'm ready to delve into programming professionally.

My forte

I'm an avid javascript programmer. I started coding in 2012 and quickly worked through the whole codecademy curriculum. Learning a bit about languages such as ruby and python gave me a decent understanding of object-oriented programming principles, but I decided to concentrate on learning javascript due to its versatility.

I dabbled in creating simple websites using libraries such as jQuery and bootstrap when I first got started. A couple years later, I installed linux on a new laptop and discovered node. From there I've slowly been working on learning new libraries and frameworks. I've primarily built MEAN-stack apps using Angular, Yeoman, Express, MongoDB. Most recently, I've been learning to implement Hyperledger blockchains and networks.
